  • Surfbored is started with an idea to be a go-to place for those who would like to do something interesting in their freetime.
  • Over a year, Surfbored intends to be:
    • An Indian movie database - building its own datasets, blog and sourcing data from other websites/apps.
    • A board games hub, with interesting and completely new board games
    • A platform for users to present their views, post movie articles and build engaging community
  • Finding a wholistic review of an Indian movie is a problem. People generally visit IMDb or random articles to know about a movie.
  • Most of the Board Game websites are advertisement heavy and do not present a clean screen when games are played. That is not very convenient for the users
  • Collate real-time information about a movie at the time of its release, and present to the users whenever they need
  • Surfbored will allow Users/Gamers to play board games on a clean screen.
  • This website is started as a project to facilitate my learning.
  • Users can get points either by playing games or contributing to the development of the project through articles/otherwise. These points will be equivalent to real money.
  • Revenue streams: Movie promotions for new releases, and games built specifically promoting a brand or a company.
